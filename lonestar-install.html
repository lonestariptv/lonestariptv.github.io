Installation Guide
Follow the steps below to get Lone Star TV up and running on your device. Need help? We've got you covered.

⚠️ Before You Start: Make sure your VPN is installed, active, and set to a static IP address on your device before proceeding. A static IP is required for your account to function correctly — do not skip this step.

We recommend Proton VPN (free) or Surfshark VPN (our personal pick — unlimited devices + built-in static IP support). Once your VPN is active with a static IP, come back and continue below.
🔓
Step 1 · Firestick Only Enable Developer Mode
ℹ️ Why is this needed? Amazon restricts installing apps from outside their App Store by default. Developer Mode unlocks this so you can install Lone Star TV apps.

Not on a Firestick? Android devices and Windows PCs can skip directly to Step 3.
1
Go to Settings
Press the Home button on your Fire TV remote, then select Settings.
2
Open My Fire TV or System
Select My Fire TV or System depending on your device model.
3
Select About
Select the About option from the menu.
4
Click Your Device Name 7 Times
On the About page, select the first item (your Fire TV device's name) seven times. You'll see a countdown at the bottom of the screen. Keep clicking until you see:

"No Need, You Are Already a Developer"

Your Developer Options menu is now unlocked.
5
Go Back & Open Developer Options
Press the Back button on your remote to return to the previous menu, then select Developer Options.
6
Enable Install Unknown Apps
Select Install Unknown Apps. For each app listed, toggle the setting to On. This allows apps to be installed from outside the Amazon App Store.
📥
Step 2 · Firestick Only Install the Downloader App
1
Find & Install Downloader
Search for Downloader in the Amazon App Store and install it. It's a free app used to sideload our streaming apps onto your Firestick.
2
Open Downloader & Enter the Code
Open the Downloader app and enter this code: 250931

This will redirect you to our app library. Scroll down to find TiviMate, IPTV Smarters Pro, and NexTV. Choose your preferred app.
⚠️ Code not working? If the code 250931 doesn't work on your Firestick, enter this URL instead:

firesticktricks.com/smarter

NexTV

1
Open NexTV
Launch the app. If you see a "Software Update" prompt, press Later.
2
Add Your Playlist
Press Add Your Playlist, then select Xstream.
3
Enter Your Credentials
Fill in the fields using your welcome email:
Hostname URL from your email
Username From your email
Password From your email
4
On Mobile — Use M3U Instead
If setting up NexTV on a phone or tablet, select Add Your Playlist → M3U Playlist instead.

Enter any Playlist Name and paste your M3U Link from your email into the Server URL field. Press Next and start streaming.
5
Continue & Start Streaming
Hit Continue and your content will load. You're good to go!
